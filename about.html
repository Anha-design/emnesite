ALL THINGS MOVIE

Forside / About us

Every story has a beginning

All Things Movies started with a simple idea:

movies aren’t just about critics or fancy reviews, they’re about how they make us feel. Some of my best memories are sitting with friends, talking late into the night about the films that made us laugh, cry, or jump out of our seats. I wanted to create a place that captured that same feeling.

Here, it’s not the critics who decide what matters. It’s you, it’s me, and it’s everyone who loves movies just as much as we do. This is a space where you can share your passion, discover someone else’s all-time favorite, and maybe even find a new one of your own.

All Things Movies is for fans, by fans.  Because in the end, movies don’t belong to critics,  they belong to us.
